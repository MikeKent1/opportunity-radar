Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Prizen handles information when you use the app and related website.

Information we collect

Prizen may collect account information provided through Google sign-in, such as your email address, name, profile image, and authentication identifier. We also store app activity needed to provide features, such as opportunities you save.

How we use information

  • To let you sign in and access your Prizen account.
  • To save and show opportunities you choose to keep.
  • To maintain, secure, debug, and improve the app.
  • To respond to support requests.

Third-party services

Prizen uses Supabase for backend services and Google sign-in for authentication. Opportunity links may open third-party websites. Those websites are controlled by their own operators and have their own privacy practices.

Data retention

We keep account and saved opportunity data while your account remains active or as needed to operate the service. You can request account deletion at any time.

Your choices

You can remove saved opportunities in the app. To request deletion of your account and associated data, follow the instructions on the Delete Account page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@prizen.app.
