Support

How can we help?

For app support, account questions, data requests, or issue reports, contact the Prizen team by email.

Email support

Send a message to support@prizen.app. This inbox is being prepared for launch, so response times may vary during beta.

Helpful details to include

  • The email address used for your Prizen account.
  • Your device model and operating system version.
  • A short description of what happened.
  • Screenshots, if they help explain the issue.

Account deletion

If you want to delete your account and associated data, visit the Delete Account page.
